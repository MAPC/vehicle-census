MA Vehicle Census

The Massachusetts Vehicle Census catalogs information about nearly every vehicle registered in the Commonwealth of Massachusetts from 2009 to 2014, while protecting personally identifiable information.

This data product supports analyses of the factors that influence patterns of auto ownership and use, and helps to track the impact of policies which promote sustainable transportation.
